How UTMB Qualification Works

Two Requirements for the CCC Lottery

1. Running Stones (lottery tickets) — earned by finishing UTMB World Series races:

CategoryEvent StonesMajor Stones (2x)
20K12
50K24
100K36
100M48
  • Stones never expire, but go inactive if no new stone earned in 24 months
  • When selected in lottery, all stones reset to zero
  • More stones = more entries = better odds

2. Valid UTMB Index — for CCC, need a finish in the 50K, 100K, or 100M category within 24 months. Over 5,500 races worldwide qualify.

Key Dates for CCC 2027

  • 2026: Race UTMB World Series events to collect stones and earn your Index
  • Dec 2026: Pre-register for CCC 2027 lottery (all stones must be earned by then)
  • Mid-Jan 2027: Lottery results announced
  • Aug 29, 2027: CCC race day in Chamonix, France

2025 Lottery Stats (CCC)

  • ~6,000 applicants for ~1,200 lottery spots
  • Demand ~5x capacity (growing ~20-30% per year)
  • Average applicant: 4.4 stones
  • Average selected runner: 5.7 stones
  • Recommended target: 6-8 stones
